## Facilities

### 3D Printing & Fabrication

- Object Connex2 mixed-plastic 3D printer (20 µm resolution)
- Universal Laser System VLS3.50
- Post-processing equipment and materials for 3D-printed imaging optics

### Optical Dark Room

- Multiple optical tables
- Range of lenses, mirrors, and light sources
- Mirrorcle MEMS mirrors

### Sensors

- **Thermal cameras** — FLIR A6750sc MWIR (cooled), Gobi-640-GigE (uncooled)
- **Depth sensors** — SF02 Laser Rangefinder, Velodyne HDL-32E, Kinect V2
- **High-speed cameras** — Fastcam SA-X2
- **Hyperspectral cameras** — Headwall NanoHyperspec
